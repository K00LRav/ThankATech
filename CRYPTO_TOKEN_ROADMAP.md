## Phase 3: Token Economics (How Value Grows)

### Demand Drivers

#### 1. Staking Rewards
- Customers stake THNK to get discounts on TOA purchases
- Technicians stake THNK to boost profile visibility
- Staking locks supply ‚Üí scarcity ‚Üí value ‚Üë

#### 2. Buyback & Burn
- 5-10% of TOA transaction fees (Stripe processing fees) buy THNK from market
- Tokens burned (destroyed) ‚Üí reduced supply ‚Üí value ‚Üë
- **Note:** TOA stays fiat, but platform fees fund THNK buybacks

#### 3. Governance Rights
- THNK holders vote on platform decisions
- Feature priorities, dispute resolution
- Gives token utility beyond speculation

#### 4. Premium Features
- Technicians pay THNK for:
  - Featured listings
  - Analytics dashboard
  - Verified badges (the real ones!)
- Burns tokens or adds to treasury

#### 5. Partner Integrations
- Tool suppliers accept THNK
- Training platforms give discounts for THNK
- Creates real-world utility
